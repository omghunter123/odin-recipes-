Hummus

Description

A dish originated from the middle east that. It is creamy, smooth, and full of flavor, making a perfect snack or a side for a meal. It is great for dipping, spreading or just enjoying on its own, and adds a delcious touch to your table every time.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Tahini
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon Juice
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Paprika
  • Parsley
    •

Steps

  1. Grab the chickpeas and blend them until smooth
  2. Add tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and salt
  3. Blend again until creamy
  4. Adjust consistency with water if needed
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning
  6. Serve in a bowl and optionally garnish with paprika and parsley
