Matcha Mochi

Description

A dessert originated from Japan that. It is soft, chewy, and lightly sweet, making it a perfect snack or a treat after a meal. It is great for enjoying on its own or sharing with friends, and adds a delightful touch to your dessert table every time.

Ingredients

  • Glutinous rice flour
  • Matcha powder
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cornstarch
    •

Steps

  1. Mix the glutinous rice flour, matcha powder, sugar, and water until smooth
  2. Heat the mixture until it thickens and becomes sticky
  3. Stir continuously to prevent lumps
  4. Let the mochi cool slightly
  5. Dust a surface with cornstarch and place the mochi on it
  6. Shape or cut the mochi into circular pieces.
