Chili Paneer

Description

A dish originated from India that is spicy, crispy, and full of bold flavor, making it a perfect side snack or side for a meal. It is great for sharing or enjoying on its own, and adds a delicious, fiery touch to your table every time.

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Cornflour
  • All-purpose flour
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Green chillies
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chili Sauce
  • Tomato Ketchup
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Blackpepper
  • Oil
    •

Steps

  1. Cut the paneer into cubes.
  2. Coat the paneer with cornflour and all-purpose flour.
  3. Fry the paneer until golden and crispy
  4. Sauté garlic and green chilies in oil
  5. Add onions and bell peppers and stir-fry briefly
  6. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, tomato ketchup, vinegar, salt, and pepper
  7. Toss the fried paneer into the sauce and mix well
    8.
  8. Serve hot
